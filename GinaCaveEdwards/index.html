About

My name is Gina Cave Edwards and my inner hair color has always been pink. Currently, I’m rocking a fun bleach blonde pixie cut, but the pink could make a comeback soon… I’ve been in the hair “biz” for over 20 years in Austin, Texas. I specialize in color correction and keratin treatments but I love to do it all. My true passion is giving my clients beautiful hairstyles that are low maintenance and fun. My other true passion (tied for first) is being a mom. When I’m not in my salon, I’m trying to keep up with the coolest 4 year old on the planet. The life-long relationships I develop with my clients make my job so rewarding and fun.Currently, I am accepting new clients so book an appointment here and come play!

See you soon!
-Gina
